Toast Recipe

Today, we're going to learn how to make toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • A toaster
  • Patience

Instructions

  1. Take your two slices of bread and put them inside the toaster.
  2. Now, make sure the bread is toasting.
  3. Have some patience and wait for around a couple of minutes for the bread to successfully toast.
  4. While your bread is toasting, find a plate and some other optional additions to your bread. [butter, etc]
  5. After your bread has toasted, put it on your plate and put the optional additions onto the pieces of toast.
  6. Enjoy.
